Tayler Pacific Latitude

An updated version of the classic Bridge & Burn Loren short sleeved shirt dress, the Tayler is cut to a longer midi-length and features bust darts, a single chest patch pocket, and a high front slit. With a slightly A-line fit and rear yoke box pleat, here it's rendered in a breathable linen-cotton blend for warmer times. In leafy, indigo Pacific Latitude print. 50% Linen, 50% Cotton.
