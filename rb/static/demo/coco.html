Souled-Out singer Jhené Aiko and LA-based fashion label Lovers + Friends recently launched their capsule collection/collaboration. The unique collection is inspired by Jhene’s bohemian and classic eclectic style.

The line is compromised of jumpsuits, dresses, lingerie and more. Prices range from $64-$210 and the line is now available on REVOLVE.
