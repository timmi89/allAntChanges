    1. Tip 1 of 3: A Hollywood Halloween Party

      With the Large 6 sheets, stack two pieces of cardboard together. You’re going to trace the letters, B-O-O (per 2 sheets) and then cut through the two layers of cardboard at the same time. Cut strips of cardboard side of letters.Tape the seams of the letter, and make sure it’s one solid piece.

    2. Tip 2 of 3: A Hollywood Halloween Party

      Grab your streamers and scissors and notch up the edges to create the fringe. Start at the bottom and with the glue stick, apply a solid line to attach the streamer fringe. Repeat this step by adding glue and another layer of fringe on top until you’re all done.

    3. Tip 3 of 3: A Hollywood Halloween Party

      Cut an opening at the back of the letters so you can fill it with candy. Once filled, seal it up with more streamer fringe. Once you fringed up your letters, cut two holes at the top and thread your bakers twine through. Do this to all three letters.
