Teen bitten by shark off South Carolina's Murrells Inlet

Posted 2:46 p.m. today
Updated 5 minutes ago

Get a new code
You must enter the characters with black color that stand out from the other characters

More on this

 Find News Near Me

Murrells Inlet, S.C. — A teen boy was bitten by a shark in his hand and leg Thursday at South Carolina's Murrells Inlet in Georgetown County.

Authorities told local news organizations that his injuries were not life-threatening

The fifteen-year-old was released from the hospital Thursday evening.

A cold front arrived in North Carolina early Thursday, and it will continue to sit over the area for the next several days, interacting with an area of low pressure that is pulling moisture up from the Gulf of Mexico.

Gardner said outdoor activities through the weekend, including the slate of high school football games, could be canceled or rescheduled. Numerous events have already been changed.
