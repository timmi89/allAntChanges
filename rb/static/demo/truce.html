Made from Dyneema-reinforced denim, these special edition Drop Liner packs are as durable as they are stylish.

Our Drop Liner Pack is perfect for travel, cycling, day hikes or the daily grind. With a seam-sealed interior liner, you can rest assured your items inside will stay dry. Please note - although water-resistant, the top lid pocket is NOT waterproof and care should be taken to avoid putting moisture-sensitive items in this location during wet conditions.

The moisture-wicking lumbar pad rests easy on your back, ensuring comfort when carrying a load. Our comfortable straps keep the pack close, while strap keepers ensure no webbing flaps at higher speeds. All packs come with a webbing hip strap for additional stability; an optional padded, matching hip belt is easily attached into place for extra comfort and weight distribution.

Our Drop Liner is also versatile: By tucking in the shoulder straps under the lumbar pad, your pack is now converted to ‘carry on mode,’ in which no loose straps are left hanging. By using Cilogear’s Dee-Clip strap system, you can easily compress the load in your pack, or attach extra gear that won’t fit inside; each pack comes with two Dee-Clip straps.

Closed with a 1½" buckle, our backpack is available in three different sizes and custom interior coloring. Upgrade to the Austrian-made Cobra buckle here!

Features Include:

  • Two exterior water-resistant zippered pockets, lid pocket with swivel key clip
  • Interior drop pocket and zippered pocket
  • Highly-visible reflective tape along bottom, sides, and bottom of straps
  • Thin lumbar pad adds comfort, allows for addition of hip belt, conversion to ‘carry on mode’
  • Adjustable sternum strap (buckle doubles as a whistle)
  • Cilogear’s unobtrusive Dee-Clip compression system makes it easy to attach larger items, or compress your pack
  • ¾” webbing loop inside for hydration bladders/Camelbaks (outside of drop liner)
  • 1 ½” metal Dee-Ring on end main closure buckle strap makes tightening easier
  • Strap keepers on all webbing keeps straps from flapping at higher speeds
  • Approximate empty weight 955g (2.1 lbs) - Weights vary depending on fabric used

Small:

  • 14 Liter capacity (850 Cubic Inches)
  • 10" wide x 5" deep x 17" tall (25.4cmx12.7cmx43cm)

Medium:

  • 23 Liter capacity (1400 Cubic Inches)
  • 11" wide x 7" deep x 18" tall (28cmx17.8cmx45.7cm)

Large:

  • 33 Liter capacity (2020 Cubic Inches)
  • 12" wide x 8" deep x 21" tall (30.5cmx20.3cmx53.3cm)
