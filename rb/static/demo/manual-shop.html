Story

A versatile piece designed to keep your core warm.

With 3M Thinsulate Insulation, the Range Reversible vest will keep you toasty while it remains compact and thin. Wear it with the “tech side” out, and you’re ready for camping or outdoor exploration. A wind-flap covering the center front zipper protects you from windchill and two snap button pockets keep your belongings secure. Switch to wearing it with the “wool-side” out and you’re immediately ready to catch dinner in the city or step into the office. This side also features pockets to keep your hands warm and a slanted rear pocket for added convenience.

Ideal for wearing over a dress shirt or under heavier outerwear, the Range Vest provides ultimate versatility and comfort on those brisk or frigid days.
