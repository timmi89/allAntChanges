Welcome to Jax & Co.

Located in the heart of historical South Norwalk CT, Jax & Co. Low Country Kitchen provides a destination for ready-to-eat scrumptious, high-quality foods whether for solo dining or a party, and everything in between.

Highlights include a variety of salads and prepared dishes from throughout the southern state region in the Low Country counter. Select from an array sliced-to-order meats, artisanal and specialty handcrafted cheeses from local farms and around the world. Freshly prepared foods are available hot or cold to eat in the store or take home.

This vast selection makes Jax & Co. the go-to place whether grabbing a quick lunch, taking home a delicious dinner for one or more, or having Jax & Co. create a complete menu for guests. Select from Jax & Co.’s specialty sandwiches, or stock up on a week’s coffee fixings from the only Stumptown Coffee Bar in the area!
