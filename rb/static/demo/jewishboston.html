Yom Kippur 5776/2015 on the Hill with HOH

Havurah on The Hill

Tuesday, September 22 6:30pm
Wednesday, September 23 10:30pm
The Vilna Shul, Boston
18 Phillips Street
Boston, Massachusetts 02114
  • $72.00 Regular Donation (YK Only)
  • $118.00 Regular Donation (Both Holidays)
  • $54.00 Student Rate (YK Only)
  • $96.00 Student Rate (Both Holidays)
Register HereRegistration closes on September 22

This fall, Havurah on the Hill (HOH) once again hosts High Holidays services at the historic Vilna Shul on Beacon Hill in Boston.

HOHâ€™s services are traditional in style but non-denominational, egalitarian, and open to all. Services are conducted in Hebrew with English readings mixed in. As part of our learning services mission, we provide explanations and teachings throughout the services. Services are community-led, with a variety of familiar melodies and tunes that people from all backgrounds can recognize and enjoy.

Schedule of Yom Kippur observances:
- Tuesday, September 22
6:30pm sharp - Kol Nidre

- Wednesday, September 23
9:00am - 2:00pm - Shacharit, Torah, Yizkor, and Musaf
5:00pm - 7:00pm - Communal Study Session and Neila
Community shofar blast
Kosher dairy community break-fast to follow the end of services. Sponsored by the Greenfield family.

Tickets will be issued on a first come, first served basis, and only 300 tickets will be issued per holiday. Please note this is a pre-paid event, and in order to be on the RSVP list, payment for this event is required by Tuesday, September 15th.

Havurah on the Hill is a proud participant in the CJP Ticket Match program.

For more information about ticket prices, registration, other information, including how you can get involved with the celebrations, please visit our website (http://www.vilnashul.org/events/event/high_holidays_2015).
