Step 1: Get the “Reaction Button”

← Drag this link to your Bookmarks Bar

Instructions for Firefox:

  1. Display your Bookmarks bar by clicking View > Toolbars > Bookmarks Toolbar
  2. Drag the “Reaction” button to your Bookmarks bar
  3. While browsing the web, click the “Reaction” button to react to anything on the page.
Need help? Give us a shout

Instructions for Chrome:

  1. Display your Bookmarks bar by clicking the Wrench Icon > Bookmarks > Show Bookmarks Bar
  2. Drag the “Reaction” button to your Bookmarks bar
  3. While browsing the web, click the “Reaction” button to react to anything on the page.
Need help? Give us a shout

Instructions for Safari:

  1. Display your Bookmarks bar by clicking View > Show Bookmarks Bar
  2. Drag the “Reaction” button to your Bookmarks bar
  3. While browsing the web, click the “Reaction” button to react to anything on the page.
Need help? Give us a shout

Instructions for Internet Explorer:

  1. Display your Favorites Bar by clicking Tools > Toolbars > Favorites Bar
  2. Right-click the “Reaction” button and select “Add to Favorites”
  3. On the pop-up window, select “Create in: Favorites Bar”
  4. While browsing the web, click the “Reaction” button to react to anything on the page.
