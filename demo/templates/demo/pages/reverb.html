              The vocals on this guy are simply on point. He has a confident sound that is really nice and cool in a way that engages you. The lyrics pull you in with the music arrangement and you get ensnared by the ambiance of the song. It has a great upbeat tempo that is really hot. The singer breaks it down and it goes into a bridge that is really lovely. This singer has talent that really is a cut above the rest. Just really hip music that is beautiful.
