Signature Sound

Superior noise isolation and a fully-balanced sound profile lets you experience the rich sound from every complex layer of all of your music.

Design & Materialization

Fit & Comfort

A three-axis pivot, combined with a low-compression fit, allows for consistent comfort over time.

Inspiration

Designed in our New York City studio, the Linea Collection builds its foundation on the linear geometry and architecture of the city with its clean lines and simple forms. The Collection draws inspiration from faceted precious metals, smooth shapes and classic, neutral tones.
