When it comes to protecting and storing your data, it's not a place to skimp on. However that doesn't mean you should pay an arm and leg. Comparing prices and seeing what others paid can ensure that you're paying a fair price. Below are what our customers paid. Find out if backing up your data makes sense for your business.

Pricing varies based on the features you choose- such as real-time backup or training. The level of customer service you need can also impact price: if you need 24/7 phone support, for instance, it may increase your costs.
